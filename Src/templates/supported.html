MetalSlugFontReborn Character Support

This guide lists the characters supported by MetalSlugFontReborn across its five different fonts.

Font 1 Support

  • Letters: Lowercase and Uppercase
  • Numbers: 0 to 9
  • Symbols: , * {} () ^ : $ = ! > - ∞ < # % . + & ? " ; / ~ _ | ¥ ⛶ © ♥ ▲ ▼ ◀ ▶ ⋆ ★ ☞
